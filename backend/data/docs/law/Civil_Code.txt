The Civil Code of Nepal
Part 1 - General Provisions

Section 1: Title and Commencement
This Act may be cited as the Civil Code, 2074. It shall come into force immediately.

Section 2: Definitions
Unless the subject or context otherwise requires, in this Code:
'Property' means any movable or immovable asset.
'Person' includes natural and legal persons.

Section 3: Equality before Civil Law
Everyone shall be equal before the civil law. No one shall be discriminated against in civil matters on the basis of gender, caste, or religion.

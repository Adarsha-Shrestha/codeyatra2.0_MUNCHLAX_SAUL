The Criminal Code of Nepal
Part 2 - Offenses Against Property

Section 10: Theft
Whoever intentionally takes away the movable property of another person without their consent commits theft.

Section 11: Punishment for Theft
A person who commits theft shall be liable to a term of imprisonment not exceeding 3 years and a fine not exceeding 30,000 Rupees.

Section 12: Robbery
If theft is committed by causing fear of injury to any person, it amounts to robbery. The punishment for robbery is imprisonment for up to 7 years.
